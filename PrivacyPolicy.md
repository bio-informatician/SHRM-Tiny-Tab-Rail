# Privacy Policy 

SHRM – Tiny Tab Rail Overlay respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the extension.

## 1. Data Collection

The extension requires access to your open tabs and browser windows to display a persistent sidebar.

No personal information, browsing history, or website content is collected, stored, or transmitted.

The extension does not use any analytics, tracking, or third-party services.

## 2. Permissions

* tabs – used to read the list of open tabs to populate the sidebar.

* windows – used to access tabs in each window for sync.

* scripting – used to inject the sidebar overlay into pages.

* storage – used only to save your sidebar settings (e.g., pinned tabs).

## 3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

All information used by the extension remains local to your browser.

## 4. Security

The extension follows standard Chrome security practices.

No data leaves your browser unless you explicitly choose to share it.

## 5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to comply with legal requirements or improve transparency.

Updates will be posted on the extension homepage (e.g., GitHub).
